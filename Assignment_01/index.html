Welcome to Volunteer Connect

What we do?

Volunteer Connect helps build a stronger, more thoughtful community through innovative programs that inspire, inform, and connect volunteers with the organizations that need them. Through our online platform, we connect people of all ages to valuable volunteer opportunities to build social capital in the communities we serve. To date, we have connected volunteers to over 400 of our non-profit members across Markham, Richmond Hill, and Vaughan.

These programs and services include but are not limited to:

  • Jobs, skills and career and Youth
  • Seniors and older adults
  • Family and children
  • Newcomer support services
  • Arts, culture and heritage
  • Sports and recreation
  • Clubs and organizations
